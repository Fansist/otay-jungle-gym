About the Gym

A Hidden-Gem Training Facility in East Otay Mesa

Otay Jungle Gym is a multi-service training facility housing Muay Thai, boxing, strength & conditioning, bootcamp, kettlebell, and TRX suspension training, plus sport performance programs, all under one roof for novice through professional athletes.

Long-time members describe every coach as incredibly welcoming, whether you're training for general fitness or preparing to compete, with owner Zac and Muay Thai coach Rolando singled out by name for their dedication to the local training community.

The facility includes a large traditional weight room and a dedicated turf room for conditioning work.

Questions

Good to Know Before You Show Up

Is Otay Jungle Gym good for beginners?
Yes. The facility trains novice through professional athletes across Muay Thai, boxing, strength & conditioning, and more, with coaches described by long-time members as welcoming to people training for general fitness as well as those preparing to compete.
What kind of training is offered?
Muay Thai, boxing, strength & conditioning, bootcamp, kettlebell training, TRX suspension training, and sport performance programs, supported by a large traditional weight room and a dedicated turf room.
Who should I ask for when I call?
Owner Zac and Muay Thai coach Rolando are both named directly by long-time members for their dedication to the local training community, either is a good place to start.
Where is the gym located?
Otay Jungle Gym is on the east side of Otay Mesa, San Diego, at 1562 Lancaster Point Way, San Diego, CA 92154.
What's the best way to reach the gym?
Call or text (619) 257-5838, or stop by in person, the gym's own web presence is limited, so phone and Yelp/Instagram are the most reliable ways to connect.
